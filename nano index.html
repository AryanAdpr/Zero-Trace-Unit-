Zero Trace Unit

Anonymous • Ethical • Community‑First

Zero Trace Unit (ZTU) is an independent, privacy‑driven collective of cyber professionals. We operate anonymously, but our mission is public: to strengthen the safety and resilience of online communities.

We educate users on digital hygiene, research and responsibly disclose threats, and support platforms and moderators in limiting harassment, fraud, and coordinated toxicity. Our focus is on reducing the reach of toxic actors and harmful behaviors while upholding ethics and the law.

We do not chase fame or visibility. We believe true impact leaves no footprint—only a healthier, more respectful internet.
